About Us

Varmyne Studio was started as a collaborative architecture practice by architects Eshwar Raju (B.Arch), Pranav PS (B.Arch) and Sourabh DC (B.E. Civil) in the year 2019. Having studied in the same school of architecture, they shared a common design philosophy and vision. Since their practice from the outset was speculative, it was imperative to have a firm that designed as a studio.
